Welcome!

Hello! I'm Josh Bacon and you've entered my personal web app portfolio! Please explore my site, I'd love to share my passion for technology with you! You're invite to read through my articles, visit my social profiles, identify yourself via Facebook authentication, and/or provide any feedback you'd like in the below comment sections (no vulgarity please). Creating this app was great fun, and I hope you find it interesting. Behind-the-scenes is M.E..N technology stack.. MongoDB, ExpressJS, and the NodeJS runtime. I chose to ignore front-end frameworks for this project so I could focus on improving my understanding of native DOM manipulation and VanillaJS (Although I also would have enjoyed learning ReactJS for a more robust production application!). Hosting/Infrastructure for my site is backed entirely on my personal AWS account. From domain registration (R53), to DNS resolution (R53), certification management (ACM), and AWS EC2. Other technologies used include: Docker, PugJS templating engine, JSPM, NPM, CSS... Please Email me if you have any questions or interest in further discussions. Thanks for stopping by!
